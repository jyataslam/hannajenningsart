Meet Hanna Jennings

Tyler, TX

Born and raised in Texas, I have always looked for the unique. I appreciate both the bold and the understated. I strive to express myself and my vision in my art. As a Studio Art major at UT, I am thoroughly enjoying the art culture that flows throughout the city. Austin’s energy continues to influence my art in new and exciting ways. I look forward to learning and growing as an artist. I greatly admire artists like Cy Twombley, Franz Kline and Anthony Lister.

Recently, I have have begun to work with rougher mediums, like house and spray paints and scrap metal. Combining these with india ink, acrylic and pastels. I have also experimented with sewing techniques, incorporated into my canvases. I always strive for new and different ways to express my art. I dabble in architecture, fashion and graphic design, oil painting and graffiti. The world is my canvas and my inspiration.

is my canvas
and my inspiration."
